How to update your profile info?
Your name and profile photo appear on your publicly shared content in the app. You can change your profile name and photo at any time by following these steps: 1. Open the AI VisionBoard app. 2. Tap the settings icon in the bottom right corner of the screen. 3. Tap "Edit profile" at the top of the screen.
How to update your DreamMe photo?
Your DreamMe photo is a photo of your face that you can use to personalize your visionboards and place yourself in your dreams. You can update your DreamMe photo at any time by following these steps:
1. Open the AI VisionBoard app.
2. Tap the image icon in the top right corner of the screen. Alternatively, you tap the "DreamMe" button in the settings page.
3. Select and upload a photo/selfie with your face clearly visible for best results.
4. Click save, and your DreamMe photo will be updated.
5. Use your new DreamMe photo by typing "I am [dream here]" to visualize your dream self.
6. For example, try "I am a successful enterpreneur in New York". The only limit is your imagination!
7. Share your dreams with friends and family to inspire them to dream big too!

Terms and Conditions

Termination clause
A Termination clause will inform that users’ accounts on your website and mobile app or users’ access to your website and mobile (if users can’t have an account with you) can be terminated in case of abuses or at your sole discretion.
Limit what users can do
A Limit What Users Can Do clause can inform users that by agreeing to use your service, they’re also agreeing to not do certain things. This can be part of a very long and thorough list in your Terms and Conditions agreements so as to encompass the most amount of negative uses.
How to enforce Terms and Conditions
While creating and having a Terms and Conditions is important, it’s far more important to understand how you can make the Terms and Conditions enforceable. You should always use clickwrap to get users to agree to your Terms and Conditions. Clickwrap is when you make your users take some action – typically clicking something – to show they’re agreeing.
